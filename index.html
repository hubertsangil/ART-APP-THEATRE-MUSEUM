Explore the different kinds of theatres around the world

Shakespeare's Globe

The Globe Theatre was a theatre in London associated with William Shakespeare. It was built in 1599 by Shakespeare's playing company.

Bolshoi Theatre

The Bolshoi Theatre is a historic theatre in Moscow, Russia, which holds ballet and opera performances.

Sydney Opera House

The Sydney Opera House is a multi-venue performing arts centre in Sydney, Australia. It is one of the 20th century's most famous and distinctive buildings.

La Scala

La Scala, short for Teatro alla Scala, is an opera house in Milan, Italy. The theatre is regarded as one of the leading opera and ballet theatres in the world.
